Season Summary

Calabar High School is favoured to retain the Mortimer Geddes trophy on March 24th based on the results so far this season. At the very least it is a 300+ point team that is somewhat stronger than last year's squad that ended on 317 points.


Kingston College also has a strong team but one that appears somewhat weaker than the 2017 team that scored 314 points. This is mainly due to some of their strong performers from last year's champs moving up in class. To outperform then, KC will rely a lot on some first year stars - e.g. Roshawn Rowe, Ari Rodgers - doing better than projected.

Jamaica College is a little bit stronger than in 2017 and there have been rumblings of them moving in to second place. The quality is there - the depth might not be.
